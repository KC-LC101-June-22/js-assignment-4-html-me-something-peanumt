Top 10 action figures

Here are the top 10 action figures that I am proud to own.

Toy Biz Spider-Man

Here we have the original Toy Biz Spider-Man action figure. This is one of the most expensive action figures I own. I am a HUGE fan of Spider-Man, and I really enjoy have the action figure and a copy of the original comic appearance of Spider-Man. I don't plan on taking him out of the box at all.

Toy Biz Black Panther

The next figure on display is the Toy Biz Black Panther action figure. I never thought i'd own a phenomenal figure from a phenomenal comic book line. This one has a special place in my heart because I have a black superhero representation to pass down to my son.
